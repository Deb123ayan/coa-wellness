Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

At Zephyr, we offer a variety of holistic treatments including massage therapy, facial rejuvenation, aromatherapy, and hydrotherapy—all crafted to enhance your well-being.

How do I book an appointment?

Booking is simple! You can schedule your session directly through our website by clicking the "Book Now" button, or call us for a personalized appointment.

Can I customize my treatment package?

Yes, we provide flexible, customizable packages to meet your unique wellness needs. Just reach out, and we’ll tailor a plan that’s right for you.

What are your operating hours?

We’re dedicated to your relaxation and are open 24/7. Please check our contact page for more detailed information on availability and holiday hours.

Do I need to make a reservation?

Yes, we recommend scheduling your appointment in advance to ensure you secure the time that best fits your schedule.

Are your therapists certified?

Absolutely. Our team consists of licensed, highly-trained professionals committed to providing safe, effective, and personalized care.

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, you’ll enjoy a thorough consultation to understand your needs, followed by a calming treatment session in our serene, thoughtfully designed space.
