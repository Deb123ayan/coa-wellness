Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Find answers to common questions about our wellness treatments and services.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit begins with a comprehensive wellness consultation where we discuss your health history, goals, and preferences. Our expert practitioners will create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your unique needs. The session includes a tour of our facilities and an introduction to our holistic approach to wellness.

How long are the treatment sessions?

Treatment sessions vary depending on the service you choose. Our Mindful Reset sessions are 60 minutes, Body Harmony treatments last 75 minutes, and our comprehensive Holistic Renewal experience is 90 minutes. Each session includes preparation time and a brief consultation to ensure optimal results.

Do I need to prepare anything before my treatment?

Arrive 15 minutes early, wear comfortable clothing, avoid heavy meals 2 hours before, and stay hydrated.

Are your treatments suitable for beginners?

Yes! Our treatments are designed for all experience levels. Expert practitioners guide you through each step.

How often should I schedule treatments?

For maintenance, monthly sessions work well. For deeper transformation, weekly or bi-weekly treatments are recommended.

What makes COA different from other wellness centers?

COA combines ancient healing wisdom with modern techniques in a personalized approach, treating mind, body, and spirit.
