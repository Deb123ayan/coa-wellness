Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Find answers to common questions about our wellness treatments and services.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit begins with a comprehensive wellness consultation where we discuss your health history, goals, and preferences. Our expert practitioners will create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your unique needs. The session includes a tour of our facilities and an introduction to our holistic approach to wellness.

How long are the treatment sessions?

Treatment sessions vary depending on the service you choose. Our Mindful Reset sessions are 60 minutes, Body Harmony treatments last 75 minutes, and our comprehensive Holistic Renewal experience is 90 minutes. Each session includes preparation time and a brief consultation to ensure optimal results.

Do I need to prepare anything before my treatment?

We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to settle in and complete any necessary forms. Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing and avoid heavy meals 2 hours before your session. Stay hydrated throughout the day, and come with an open mind ready to embrace the healing process.

Are your treatments suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our treatments are designed for all experience levels. Our expert practitioners will guide you through each step, ensuring you feel comfortable and supported throughout your wellness journey. We customize every session to match your comfort level and experience.

How often should I schedule treatments?

The frequency depends on your wellness goals and lifestyle. For stress relief and maintenance, we recommend monthly sessions. For deeper transformation or addressing specific concerns, weekly or bi-weekly treatments may be more beneficial. Our practitioners will work with you to create an optimal schedule.

What makes COA different from other wellness centers?

COA combines ancient healing wisdom with modern wellness techniques in a truly personalized approach. Our certified practitioners focus on treating the whole person - mind, body, and spirit. We create a serene, supportive environment where every detail is designed to enhance your healing journey.
